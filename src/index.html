HERE'S A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME

Don’t worry, I’ll keep it short 🚀

3 years ago, I got to know the importance of financial literacy and how millions suffer due to lack of information. It’s a long story but somehow, this became my portal into design. Since then, I’ve tried to expand my knowledge in various related fields. I even learnt how to code and dived into topics like data analysis, intuitive design and behavioural pscychology. Everything I see, all the TV shows I watch, books I read, or people I talk; everything just comes down to me trying to see meaning in the ways humans interact with products.

To anyone reading this, I’d like to say one thing: “The world is one mighty library and there’s so much to learn.” 🤩
